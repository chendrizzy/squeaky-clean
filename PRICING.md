## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will the free version always be free?
Yes! The core CLI tool is MIT licensed and will always remain free and open-source. We're committed to keeping the Community Edition free forever.

### Can I switch plans anytime?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the end of your billing cycle.

### Do you offer discounts?
- **Annual billing**: Save 17% when you pay annually
- **Students**: 50% off Professional tier with valid .edu email
- **Open-source**: Free Professional tier for verified maintainers
- **Startups**: 30% off for companies < 2 years old

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise accounts.

### Is there a trial period?
Yes! Professional and Team tiers include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

### How does team billing work?
Team plans include 5 seats. Additional seats are $15/month each. Seats can be reassigned monthly.

### What about data privacy?
- All data stays on your machines
- We only collect anonymous usage metrics
- No source code or file contents are ever transmitted
- SOC2 Type II compliant (Enterprise)

### Can I self-host Enterprise?
Yes, self-hosted Enterprise deployments are available. Contact sales for pricing.
